Testing Demo Form

To test the demo form:

  1. Open browser to: http://localhost:3001#demo-generator
  2. Scroll to the "Try It Now - 60 Seconds" section
  3. Fill in the form:
    • Brand Name: Test Company
    • Email: test@example.com
    • Industry: Select any
    • Website: (optional)
    • Check the consent box
  4. Click "Next: Choose Platforms"
  5. Select tone and 1-4 platforms
  6. Click "Generate AI Content"
  7. View the generated mock content

Expected Behavior:

  • ✅ Step 1 shows brand information fields
  • ✅ Required fields are validated
  • ✅ Step 2 shows tone and platform selection
  • ✅ Content generation shows loading animation
  • ✅ Generated content uses actual form data (brand name, industry, tone)
  • ✅ Multiple variants per platform are shown
  • ✅ Copy button works for each variant
